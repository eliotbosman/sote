Drop-in Sessions

Graduation Show Coordinator is available for consultation during drop-in sessions. During these consultation hours you can ask all sorts of questions about the graduation show. These consultation hours will be hosted every Tuesday from March 5 onwards.

Paint Guidelines

If you wish to paint your project space, you will need to check the Paint Guidelines. These guidelines explain which types of paint are suitable and which are not. Please make sure to follow all conditions and guidelines carefully.

Equipment Coordinator

The equipment coordinator is responsible for managing all technical equipment during the graduation show. If you need to make an appointment, you can do so by sending an email or visiting during consultation hours.

Floor Plan

The floor plan shows the layout of all available spaces. Students who have not yet registered for a space should contact the graduation show coordinator as soon as possible to secure their preferred location.
